Solar panels

Solar panels are a way of generating energy without using fosil resources. Also generating energy this way prevents the greenhouse effect which is being activated by Co2 release from changing fosil resources into usable energy. The solar panels convert sunlight into DC energy, which then is changed to AC energy, which is ready to be used.
