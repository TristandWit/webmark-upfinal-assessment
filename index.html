5 reasons why to use solar power

  1. Solar energy is free, only the sun is needed.
  2. Solar panels do not produce greenhouse gases nor toxic waste.
  3. Solar panels are incredibly reliable and have a long lifespan.
  4. Solar panels are 90% recyclable
  5. The conversion of solar energy is 100% safe
